# Demo Company Profiles

Ready-to-paste profiles for live demos. Copy the description, paste into the matcher.

---

## 1. OpenAI (AI/ML)

**Description:**
We develop and deploy advanced AI systems including large language models, image generation, and reasoning engines. Our products serve millions of developers and businesses globally through API access. We focus on making AI safe, beneficial, and widely accessible. Key areas: foundation models, reinforcement learning from human feedback, AI safety research, enterprise AI deployment.

**Expected matches:** AI grants, digital infrastructure, cybersecurity (AI safety), human-AI collaboration, responsible AI

---

## 2. Northvolt (Climate Tech / Energy)

**Description:**
We design and manufacture lithium-ion batteries for electric vehicles and energy storage systems. Based in Sweden, we're building Europe's largest battery gigafactory. Our focus is on sustainable battery production using renewable energy and recycled materials, reducing Europe's dependency on Asian battery supply chains.

**Expected matches:** Clean energy, sustainable manufacturing, circular economy, critical raw materials, electric mobility

---

## 3. Doctolib (Healthcare / Digital Health)

**Description:**
We build digital health infrastructure connecting patients with healthcare professionals across Europe. Our platform handles appointment booking, teleconsultation, and secure medical document exchange. We serve over 80 million patients and 350,000 healthcare professionals in France, Germany, and Italy, with a focus on reducing healthcare access inequality.

**Expected matches:** Digital health, AI in healthcare, health data, patient-centred care, health technology assessment

---

## 4. Wiz (Cybersecurity)

**Description:**
We provide cloud security solutions that help organizations identify and remediate critical risks across their cloud environments. Our platform scans cloud infrastructure, detects vulnerabilities, and provides actionable remediation. We protect enterprise workloads across AWS, Azure, GCP, and Kubernetes with a focus on agentless, API-driven security scanning.

**Expected matches:** Cybersecurity, cloud infrastructure, data protection, digital resilience, critical infrastructure protection

---

## 5. Exotrail (Space Tech)

**Description:**
We design and manufacture electric propulsion systems for small satellites and provide mission software for satellite fleet management. Our products enable precise orbit positioning and station-keeping for LEO satellite constellations, supporting earth observation, telecommunications, and IoT connectivity from space.

**Expected matches:** Space technology, satellite systems, earth observation, telecommunications infrastructure

---

## 6. Infarm (AgriTech)

**Description:**
We build modular vertical farming units that grow fresh produce inside grocery stores, restaurants, and distribution centres. Our cloud-connected farms use 95% less water and 90% less land than traditional agriculture while eliminating supply chain food miles. We use AI-driven growing algorithms to optimise crop yield and nutrition.

**Expected matches:** Sustainable agriculture, food systems, precision farming, circular economy, AI in agriculture

## Demo Tips

- **Start with OpenAI** — judges will immediately relate, and it showcases the AI matching at its most intuitive
- **Follow with a contrasting profile** (Northvolt or Infarm) to show the matcher works across sectors
- **If a judge offers their company** — type it live. That's the money moment
- **Point out the "why it matches" explanation** — that's the AI value, not just the list
